About K.P.S.A.G.I.C

Empowering Our Village Through Education

Established as a private college in the heart of Village Atiya Majhigawan, we are deeply committed to providing accessible, high-quality UP Board education to the girls of our rural community.

Comprehensive Learning

Offering specialized streams in Agriculture, Commerce, Science (Math/Bio), and Arts to meet diverse career aspirations.

Women Empowerment

A safe, encouraging environment tailored primarily for girls' education in the rural sector.

Community Integrated

Our Dedicated Streams

We proudly offer a diverse range of subjects to cater to the interests and future careers of our students, empowering them with practical and theoretical knowledge.

Agriculture

Admissions

Join K.P.S. Inter College

We welcome students to join our thriving educational community. Admissions are open for Hindi medium classes 6 through 12 across all streams.

1

Visit the Campus

Come to our private campus at Village Atiya Majhigawan during operating hours (Mon-Sat, 7AM-4PM) to collect the admission form.

2

Submit Documents

Submit your completed form along with previous academic records, Aadhaar card, and photographs.

3

Choose Your Stream

Select your desired subjects (Agriculture, Commerce, Arts, Math, or Biology) and complete the fee payment.

Call for Enquiry

Contact Administration

Need information about subjects, fees, or facilities? Send us a message or call our office directly.
